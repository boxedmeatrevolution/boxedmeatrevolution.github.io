About us

We're a small team of hobbyist game developers who participate in game jams, particularly Ludum Dare. We are always experimenting with every game—either through gameplay, new development tools, or overcoming technological hurdles.

Duane Byer

Duane makes sure that everyone's work comes together to make a finished game. He enjoys adding the little creative touches (mostly wobbles and jiggles) that make a game feel complete.

Aidan Mahler

Aidan's thoughtful approach to level/game design means that our games are actually fun to play. He makes sure to advocate for the player in all aspects of design (especially difficulty!). He is wicked good at designing puzzles.

Nikole McGregor
website

Nikole's art is either incredibly adorable or horrific, depending who you ask. She brings the weird (and disturbing?) ideas that inspire the whole direction—both artistically and mechanically—of our games.

George Shen

George's music makes a game's whole experience suddenly click. He's great at bringing the vibe we need, even if we don't know what it is. He also does our sound design and voice acting, including starring roles as a merlion, plant ogre, and cardboard puppet.
